The autumn in New Zealand is cool and mild. The maple tree leafs are turning red, and begin to fall of the trees. In a certain sence it is very nice to enjoy the view when people drive or walk. It is good to go to Queenstown to have a look of the beautiful scenery.

Places to see the autumn colour in New Zealand:

Mackenzie Country
Lake Tekapo and Lake Pukaki
The Waitaki Valley
Lake Benmore and Lake Aviemore
